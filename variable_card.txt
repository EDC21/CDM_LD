================================== Variables =========================================
* ulocal - displacement
*        - ulocal(1): Mode I displacement
*        - ulocal(2): Mode II displacement
* uI     - Mode I separation
* uII    - Resultant Mode II displacement
* u      - Mixed-mode disp.
* u0     - Damage initiation displacement
* umax   - u^f, displacement at failure
* FI     - Normal tensile interface stress
* FII    - Shear interface stress
